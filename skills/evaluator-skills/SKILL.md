## DFV Evaluation Guidelines

When evaluating a project, review the outputs from the Desirability, Feasibility, and Viability agents.

### Evaluation Checklist

1. **Desirability**
   - Verify that a real user problem exists.
   - Assess market demand and customer interest.
   - Review competitor landscape and differentiation.

2. **Feasibility**
   - Assess technical feasibility.
   - Review infrastructure, tools, and implementation complexity.
   - Identify operational challenges and resource requirements.

3. **Viability**
   - Evaluate business model and revenue potential.
   - Assess customer segments and market opportunity.
   - Review costs, scalability, and long-term sustainability.

### Risk Assessment

Identify and summarize:

- **Technical Risks**
  - Technology limitations
  - Scalability concerns
  - Integration challenges

- **Business Risks**
  - Market competition
  - Customer adoption barriers
  - Revenue uncertainty

- **Operational Risks**
  - Resource constraints
  - Maintenance overhead
  - Execution challenges

### Decision Rules

#### GO
Recommend GO when:
- User demand is validated.
- The solution is technically achievable.
- The business model is sustainable.
- Risks are manageable.

#### NO-GO
Recommend NO-GO when:
- Market demand is weak.
- Technical implementation is unrealistic.
- Business sustainability is doubtful.
- Critical risks remain unresolved.

### Output Format

#### Final Decision
GO / NO-GO

#### Executive Summary
- Summarize overall project readiness.
- Highlight major strengths.
- Highlight major concerns.
- Provide a concise final assessment.

#### Key Strengths
- Strong customer demand.
- Clear value proposition.
- Technical feasibility.
- Sustainable business model.

#### Key Weaknesses
- Missing customer validation.
- High implementation complexity.
- Resource constraints.
- Scalability concerns.

#### Risk Assessment
- Technical Risks: Technology limitations, integration issues.
- Business Risks: Competition, adoption barriers.
- Operational Risks: Resource and maintenance challenges.


#### DFV Summary
- Desirability
- Feasibility
- Viability

#### Improvement Suggestions
(Required only for NO-GO decisions)

### Important Rules

- Do not provide numerical scores or percentages.
- Be objective and evidence-based.
- Keep recommendations actionable and concise.
- Maintain a professional evaluation tone.
